# Lesson 1 Exercises: Reinforcement Learning Fundamentals

## Questions

1. What is reinforcement learning and how does it differ from supervised learning?

2. Explain the reward signal and its importance in RL systems.

3. What are states, actions, and transitions in a Markov Decision Process?

4. Explain the concept of a policy and value function in RL.

5. What is the difference between model-based and model-free reinforcement learning?

6. Explain the exploration-exploitation trade-off in RL.

7. What is the Bellman equation and how does it relate to value iteration?

8. Explain Q-learning and how it learns optimal policies.

9. What are temporal difference (TD) methods and their advantages?

10. What are practical applications of reinforcement learning in real-world systems?
